## Fuel Types

| Fuel | Application | Key Constraint |
|---|---|---|
| Jet-A | Turbine (US domestic) | Freeze point -40C |
| Jet-A1 | Turbine (international) | Freeze point -47C, ICAO standard |
| Avgas 100LL | Piston (GA) | NEVER in turbine aircraft — blue dye |
| Avgas UL91/UL94 | Unleaded piston | Limited airport availability |
| SAF blend | Turbine | Max 50% blend ratio; certification required |
| Jet-B / JP-4 | Military/cold climate | Rare commercially |

## FBO Minimums
- Typical turbine minimum: 25-50 USG
- Mobile fueler at small airports: varies, may have no minimum

## Density and Conversion Reference
- Jet-A: 6.71 LBS/USG at 60F / 0.804 kg/L at 15C (standard)
- Avgas 100LL: 6.00 LBS/USG
- Temperature corrections per ASTM D1250 tables
- International orders: KG or LTR; US domestic: USG or LBS

## Regulatory Minimums (Fuel Load)
- FAA Part 91: trip fuel + 45-min reserve (day VFR) / 30-min (night)
- FAA Part 121: trip fuel + alternate fuel + final reserve (45 min turbine)
- FAA Part 135: similar to 121 requirements

## Pricing Reference (2024-2025 typical spot)
- Jet-A: $4.50 - $9.00 per USG
- Avgas 100LL: $6.00 - $10.00 per USG
- SAF premium: 2-5x conventional Jet-A price
